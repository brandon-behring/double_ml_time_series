# Temporal and dynamic DML estimators

Temporal and dynamic DML estimators — the user's emphasized sub-area. Covers Lewis–Syrgkanis recursive g-estimation, dynamic treatment effects with DML/ML nuisance, panel DML with fixed effects, rolling-window / walk-forward DML, structural nested mean models, marginal structural models, and DML for time-series settings with serial correlation.
