# Cross-sectional DML estimators and heterogeneous treatment effects

Cross-sectional / i.i.d. DML estimators (PLR, IRM, IIVM) and the heterogeneous-treatment-effect (CATE) family: causal forests, generalized random forests, meta-learners (S/T/X/R/DR-learner), Bayesian causal forests, and neural-network adaptations (Dragonnet, CFR, GANITE).
